Formulas

        Trigonométrie:
        sin(θ) = Opposite / Hypotenuse
        cos(θ) = Adjacent / Hypotenuse
        tan(θ) = Opposite / Adjacent

        Pythagore:
        a² + b² = c²

        Calcul d'équation de droite:
        y = mx + b
        m = (y2 - y1) / (x2 - x1)
        b = y1 - m * x1
